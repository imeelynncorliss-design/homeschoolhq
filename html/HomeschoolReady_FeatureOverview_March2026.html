HomeschoolReady — Feature Overview & Competitive Positioning

March 2026 · Prepared for Courtney & Britany

What HomeschoolReady Is

HomeschoolReady is an AI-powered homeschool management platform built specifically for working parents who homeschool. It combines daily lesson tracking, compliance record-keeping, AI lesson planning, and a full resource library in one mobile-first app.

Full Feature Set (Current)

🤖 AI — Scout Copilot

Feature Details
Scout Chat Conversational AI assistant trained on homeschool context — lesson help, scheduling, compliance questions, curriculum advice
Lesson Generator Generates 3 full lesson plan variations per request, personalized to student’s grade, learning style, curriculum, and interests
Activity Generator Generates 3 quick activity ideas with a “vibe” selector (hands-on, game-based, brain break, review)
Curriculum Awareness Scout adapts output to named curriculum (Sonlight, Saxon Math, Classical Conversations, etc.) or eclectic/flexible approach
Learning Style Matching Activities and lessons matched to student’s documented learning style
Conversation History Starred and saved Scout conversations

📅 Planning & Scheduling

Feature Details
Lesson Builder Create, schedule, and manage lessons per subject per child
Bulk Lesson Scheduler Schedule multiple lessons at once across subjects and weeks
Calendar View Visual weekly/monthly calendar of scheduled lessons
Google Calendar Sync Connect Google Calendar to detect conflicts between work meetings and homeschool lessons
Vacation & Break Planning Set vacation periods that auto-exclude from school day counts
Auto-scheduling Smart lesson scheduling around work calendar conflicts

👩‍🎓 Student Management

Feature Details
Multi-child Support Manage multiple students, each with their own profile and records
Student Profiles Age, grade, learning style, current interests, curriculum, photo

Where HomeschoolReady Wins

1. AI That Actually Knows Homeschooling

Scout isn’t a generic AI — it knows curriculum names, teaching philosophies, learning styles, and state compliance requirements. Competitors using AI (Blossom) offer basic generation with no curriculum context or personalization depth.

2. Built for Working Parents

Google Calendar conflict detection, bulk scheduling, and a co-teacher role are purpose-built for parents managing a job alongside homeschooling. No competitor has conflict detection.

3. The Only App with State Law Built In

Every homeschool family needs to know their state’s requirements. We’re the only platform with a searchable, plain-language reference for all 50 states + DC inside the product.

4. High School Done Right

Transcript builder + course descriptions + a built-in high school planning guide (credits, SAT/ACT, college apps) makes HomeschoolReady the only app that genuinely supports families through graduation.

5. Enrichment Records That Count

Reading logs and field trip logs with print support for portfolio review states. These are compliance documents — most apps ignore them entirely.
